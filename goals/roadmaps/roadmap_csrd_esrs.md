# CSRD / ESRS Reporting Roadmap

This is the working checklist for the sustainability statement. Tick each item
(`- [ ]` → `- [x]`) once the data is collected from a **sourced document** and
reflected in `output/report.md`. Items left as `[DATA NEEDED]` in the report stay
unchecked. Do not invent data to check a box.

## 1. Engagement setup

- [ ] Reporting entity confirmed (legal name, country of incorporation, sector / NACE code)
- [ ] Reporting period / fiscal year confirmed
- [ ] Consolidation boundary confirmed (single entity vs. group; list of subsidiaries in scope)
- [ ] Employee headcount band & CSRD wave / Omnibus I threshold applicability confirmed
- [ ] Reporting framework confirmed as ESRS (Set 1) and any phase-in exemptions noted
- [ ] Prior-year sustainability report / annual report obtained for context and baselines

## 2. Double materiality assessment

- [ ] Existing double materiality assessment requested (or confirmed none exists)
- [ ] Stakeholder groups identified and engagement method recorded
- [ ] Impact materiality evaluated (inside-out: actual/potential impacts on people & environment)
- [ ] Financial materiality evaluated (outside-in: risks/opportunities affecting the undertaking)
- [ ] List of material topical standards (E1–E5, S1–S4, G1) finalised with rationale
- [ ] Material/non-material determination documented in the report (provisional clearly labelled)

## 3. ESRS 2 — General disclosures (always mandatory)

- [ ] BP-1 / BP-2: basis for preparation, scope, and any use of phase-in provisions
- [ ] GOV-1: administrative/management/supervisory bodies — composition & diversity
- [ ] GOV-2/GOV-3: how sustainability is overseen and embedded in incentive schemes
- [ ] GOV-4: due diligence statement mapping
- [ ] GOV-5: risk management & internal controls over sustainability reporting
- [ ] SBM-1: strategy, business model, and value chain described
- [ ] SBM-2: stakeholder interests and views
- [ ] SBM-3: material impacts, risks & opportunities and their interaction with strategy
- [ ] IRO-1: process to identify and assess material impacts, risks & opportunities
- [ ] IRO-2: list of ESRS disclosure requirements covered
- [ ] MDR (policies, actions, targets, metrics) structure applied to each material topic

## 4. E1 — Climate change (if material)

- [ ] Energy consumption total (MWh) with mix by source, from utility/fuel records
- [ ] Scope 1 emissions (tCO2e) with activity data and emission factors cited
- [ ] Scope 2 emissions (tCO2e), both location-based and market-based
- [ ] Scope 3 emissions (tCO2e) by relevant category, with methodology
- [ ] GHG intensity metric(s) (e.g. per revenue / per FTE)
- [ ] Transition plan, climate targets & baseline year, and progress
- [ ] Energy/emission emission factors externally verified (DEFRA/IEA/GHG Protocol)

## 5. Other environmental topics (only those assessed material)

- [ ] E2 Pollution: emissions to air/water/soil, substances of concern
- [ ] E3 Water & marine resources: withdrawal, discharge, consumption (m³)
- [ ] E4 Biodiversity & ecosystems: sites in/near sensitive areas, impact drivers
- [ ] E5 Resource use & circular economy: material inflows/outflows, waste & diversion rate

## 6. S1 — Own workforce (if material)

- [ ] Total headcount by gender, country, and contract type (permanent/temporary, full/part-time)
- [ ] Diversity metrics: gender split of management, age distribution, disability
- [ ] Collective bargaining coverage and social dialogue
- [ ] Health & safety: recordable injury rate / LTIR, fatalities, near-misses
- [ ] Training: average hours per employee, programmes
- [ ] Remuneration: gender pay gap, ratio of highest paid to median
- [ ] Turnover and new-hire rates

## 7. Other social topics (only those assessed material)

- [ ] S2 Workers in the value chain: supplier code, audits, grievances
- [ ] S3 Affected communities: community impacts and engagement
- [ ] S4 Consumers & end-users: product safety, data privacy impacts

## 8. G1 — Business conduct (if material)

- [ ] Anti-corruption & anti-bribery policy and training coverage
- [ ] Confirmed incidents, fines, or sanctions (or confirmation of none)
- [ ] Whistleblowing mechanism and number of reports
- [ ] Supplier payment practices and management of supplier relationships
- [ ] Political engagement / lobbying disclosures

## 9. Fact-check & finalise

- [ ] Every quantitative figure attributed to a specific source document and location
- [ ] Cross-document consistency checked (no conflicting figures across uploads)
- [ ] Arithmetic recomputed (totals, intensities, percentages)
- [ ] `fact-checker` subagent run and its CONTRADICTED/UNCERTAIN findings resolved
- [ ] All exact ESRS datapoint IDs verified against official EFRAG texts
- [ ] Remaining gaps clearly marked `[DATA NEEDED: ...]` (not fabricated)
- [ ] Report exported / presented to the user
