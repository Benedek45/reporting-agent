# Voluntary ESG / Sustainability Report Roadmap

This is the working checklist for the report. Tick each item (`- [ ]` → `- [x]`)
once the data is collected from a **sourced document** and reflected in
`output/report.md`. Items left as `[DATA NEEDED]` stay unchecked. Do not invent
data to check a box.

## 1. Engagement setup

- [ ] Reporting entity confirmed (legal name, sector, ownership structure)
- [ ] Reporting period confirmed
- [ ] Reporting boundary confirmed (single entity vs. group; sites included)
- [ ] Framework(s) chosen (GRI 2021 default; SASB / ISSB / blend as requested)
- [ ] Primary audience identified (stakeholders / investors / regulators / public)
- [ ] Prior sustainability report / annual report obtained for context & baselines

## 2. Materiality (GRI 3)

- [ ] Existing materiality assessment requested (or confirmed none exists)
- [ ] Stakeholder engagement process and groups documented
- [ ] Impact materiality determined (organisation's impacts on economy, environment, people)
- [ ] Financial materiality added if audience is investors (enterprise-value lens)
- [ ] Final list of material topics agreed and recorded (provisional clearly labelled)

## 3. Organisational profile & governance (GRI 2)

- [ ] Legal name, ownership, headquarters, markets served
- [ ] Scale: employees, operations, revenue
- [ ] Governance structure and board composition
- [ ] Board/management oversight of ESG / sustainability
- [ ] Membership of associations and external commitments

## 4. Environmental (only topics assessed material)

- [ ] Energy consumption & mix (GRI 302) from utility/fuel records
- [ ] GHG emissions Scope 1 (GRI 305) with activity data & factors
- [ ] GHG emissions Scope 2 (location- and market-based)
- [ ] GHG emissions Scope 3 by relevant category (where material)
- [ ] Emission intensity metric(s)
- [ ] Water withdrawal & discharge (GRI 303)
- [ ] Waste generated & diverted (GRI 306)
- [ ] Biodiversity impacts for sensitive sites (GRI 304)
- [ ] Emission/energy factors externally verified (DEFRA/IEA/GHG Protocol)

## 5. Social (only topics assessed material)

- [ ] Workforce composition: headcount by gender, region, contract type (GRI 2-7 / 401)
- [ ] Diversity of governance bodies & employees (GRI 405)
- [ ] Health & safety: injury rate / LTIR, fatalities, near-misses (GRI 403)
- [ ] Training & development hours per employee (GRI 404)
- [ ] Turnover and new hires (GRI 401)
- [ ] Human rights due diligence & supplier social assessment (GRI 407–414)
- [ ] Community engagement / local impact

## 6. Governance (only topics assessed material)

- [ ] Ethics & anti-corruption policy and incidents (GRI 205)
- [ ] Anti-competitive behaviour incidents (GRI 206)
- [ ] Whistleblowing mechanism and reports
- [ ] Data privacy & security approach
- [ ] Board independence & diversity

## 7. Targets & performance

- [ ] Each material topic has a metric with a baseline year
- [ ] Targets stated with target year
- [ ] Year-over-year progress shown vs. baseline
- [ ] Missed/exceeded targets explained

## 8. Appendix & finalise

- [ ] Methodology notes and key assumptions documented
- [ ] GRI / SASB content index with disclosure codes (verified against official standards)
- [ ] Detailed data tables included
- [ ] Every figure attributed to a specific source document and location
- [ ] Cross-document consistency checked (no conflicting figures)
- [ ] Arithmetic recomputed (totals, intensities, percentages)
- [ ] `fact-checker` subagent run and CONTRADICTED/UNCERTAIN findings resolved
- [ ] Remaining gaps marked `[DATA NEEDED: ...]` (not fabricated)
- [ ] Report exported / presented to the user
